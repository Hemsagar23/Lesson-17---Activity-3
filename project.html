My Favourite Fruits.

Dragon Fruit

A dragon fruit is a magenta colored fruit. The inner part is white color and contains small black seeds.

Mango

A Mango is an orange/yellow colored fruit. The inner contains a huge seed and eatable can be white(if raw) and yellow(if ripe).

Custard Aplple

A Custard Apple is a green colored fruit. The inner contains a lot of small oval like black seeds covered with eatables.
